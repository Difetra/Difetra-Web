What I’ll draw

There’s not a whole lot I won’t draw, but don’t feel bad If I say no, I don’t judge.
What I won’t draw: scat, diapers, under aged characters(nsfw)
Anything else is a case by case basis. I’ll let you know if I can’t do it.

Payment

Payment will be done through paypal. Before starting a commission I’ll request your email so I can send an invoice for the amount owed. Once payment has been received I will begin work. If for some reason I’m unable to complete the commission the full amount will be refunded. If you cancel the commission a partial refund may be given out based on the amount of work completed.

Time

Commissions should be completed within 2 weeks of payment. This time will depend on the type of commission, current workload, and life. 2 weeks is a (hopefully) safe cushion for anything unexpected, completion could be much sooner. If there is a delay I’ll be sure to notify you.

The Process

This varies based on the type of commission. For more detailed works I’ll offer an initial sketch for approval and make adjustments as necessary, once satisfied I’ll finish the rest of the piece. For larger paintings I can provide additional previews if requested. For sketches/speed paintings it’s not practical to offer any kind of preview, but I can make minor adjustments once finished.

Use of Artwork

You may:

  • repost your commissioned art to any gallery with appropriate credit given to the artist - Difetra.
  • Make prints for private use
  • Use as an avatar, banner, or background on any website.

You may not:

  • profit from or resell artwork

I retain rights to the commissioned artwork and may post it to my galleries unless negotiated otherwise.
